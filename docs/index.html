Polygon: click points; double-click, Enter or Close finishes the contour. Freehand: hold and draw the contour. Magnetic: hold and draw; points snap to nearby image edges. After closing the contour, Copy / Cut and Take selection become active. After Take selection, Move, Rotate, Scale, Flip, Flop and Delete become active.
Resize changes the real output dimensions. With aspect ratio ON, Height follows Width and Width follows Height. With aspect ratio OFF, Width and Height are independent.
